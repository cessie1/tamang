HGB COLLEGE

OUR MISSION

HGBaquiran College commits itself to offer quality education that would develop a well rounded human being who possesses desirable values, attitudes and skills thereby enabling him/her to be locally competitive, live a productive life, cope with the social and moral challenges of the modern world and be able to contribute to nation building.

Our VISION

A well rounded human being. Equipped with the values, attitudes and skills that will enable him/her to be locally and globally competitive, live a productive life, contribute to nation building, and meet creatively and resourcefully the social and moral challenges of the world

CORE VALUES

Dedication, Diligence, Determination, Devotion and Discipline.
